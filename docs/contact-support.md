# Contact and support

The repo currently defines product flows and legal pages, but it does **not** currently commit a public support inbox or contact form.

For now, use the support path that already exists inside the product flow, and treat the public contact details below as a launch checklist item for the maintainer.

## Best current routing

### Account or sign-in issues

- start at [app.chorehero.cloud/login](https://app.chorehero.cloud/login)
- reproduce the issue from a fresh sign-in path
- capture the exact step that failed

### Billing questions

- open the parent app
- go to **Parent Dashboard > Settings > Account**
- use the available billing or customer-portal actions first

### Privacy and legal questions

- [Privacy policy](https://app.chorehero.cloud/privacy-policy)
- [Terms of use](https://app.chorehero.cloud/terms-of-use)

## What to include when asking for help

Share:

- whether the issue affects a parent or child flow
- the household action you were trying to complete
- whether the issue started after a billing, onboarding, or settings change
- screenshots or exact error text when available

:::note For the maintainer
Before public launch, replace this article with a monitored support email address, contact form, or help desk path that matches your production support process.
:::
