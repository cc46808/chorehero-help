# ChoreHero Help Center

Use this site when you need the parent-facing explanation of a feature, flow, or plan change.

## Best first links

- New household or first login: [Getting started](./getting-started.md)
- Common parent questions: [Parent FAQs](./parent-faqs.md)
- Plans, billing, and subscriptions: [Billing and subscriptions](./billing-subscriptions.md)
- Child access and onboarding: [Account, login, and onboarding](./account-login-onboarding.md)
- Day-to-day chores and rewards: [Chores and rewards basics](./chores-rewards-basics.md)
- Fast fixes: [Troubleshooting](./troubleshooting.md)

## What belongs here

- Parent-facing setup steps
- Billing and subscription guidance
- Explanations of child access, quick login, approvals, chores, rewards, and troubleshooting
- Stable support language that should stay in sync with product behavior

## What does not belong here

- Internal deployment runbooks
- Engineering architecture notes
- Secrets, operator-only workflows, or troubleshooting that depends on private infrastructure access

Those stay in the repo root `docs/` folder.
